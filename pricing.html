Pricing and Fees

Please check my availability before submitting a ride request. This can be accomplished by clicking over to the availability tab. Any ride requests for a time that is not offered will be denied. Please note that no rides will be offered between 12AM and 5:30AM under any circumstance, but my availability will change from week to week, so check it every time you request a ride. You will be notified of whether or not your ride request has been approved within 24 hours maximum and likely much earlier via your preferred mode of contact.

$20 To and from New Haven Union Station ($25 for two or more people)

$30 Per car to and from Bradley International Airport

$20 To and from Hartford Bus Terminal ($25 for two or more people)

$20 Per hour to and from storage units

The former price of $15 per hour will still be applicable to low income students. I will gladly help you move and carry boxes. Low income students receive 30% minimum off of rides to all other locations, no questions asked.
